## üé£ Hook Categories

### 1. Curiosity Gap Hooks

Create an information gap that DEMANDS closure:

```
"The [thing] nobody tells you about [topic]..."
"I tested [thing] for [X] days. Here's what happened..."
"Why is nobody talking about this?"
"The real reason [surprising claim]..."
"What if I told you [counterintuitive statement]?"
"Here's what [authority figures] don't want you to know..."
"The [X] trick that [impressive result]..."
```

**Best For**: Education, Tips, Reviews
**Platform Fit**: All platforms

---

### 2. Controversy/Hot Take Hooks

Provoke reaction with bold statements:

```
"Unpopular opinion: [controversial but defensible take]"
"[Popular thing] is overrated. Here's why..."
"Everyone is wrong about [topic]..."
"I'm going to make some people angry..."
"This might get me cancelled, but..."
"Stop doing [common practice]. It doesn't work."
"The [topic] industry is lying to you."
```

**Best For**: Commentary, Reviews, Opinions
**Platform Fit**: TikTok (strongest), Reels, Shorts

---

### 3. Pattern Interrupt Hooks

Break expected patterns to stop the scroll:

```
"WAIT. Before you scroll..."
"POV: [unexpected scenario]"
"Story time: [dramatic pause]"
"STOP. This is important."
"3... 2... 1... [transition to content]"
"[Silent pause] ...okay now that I have your attention..."
```

**Best For**: Entertainment, Stories, News
**Platform Fit**: TikTok (best), Reels

---

### 4. Value Promise Hooks

Immediate, clear value proposition:

```
"3 things I wish I knew before [X]..."
"The fastest way to [achieve result]..."
"How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]..."
"[X] tips that will change how you [action]..."
"The only [thing] you need for [goal]..."
"Here's exactly how I [achievement]..."
"Save this for later üìå"
```

**Best For**: Tutorials, Tips, How-tos
**Platform Fit**: All platforms (Reels loves "save this")

---

### 5. Relatability Hooks

Connect through shared experience:

```
"Tell me you're a [type] without telling me..."
"When you [relatable scenario]..."
"Me pretending [relatable behavior]..."
"That moment when [universal experience]..."
"Why does [frustrating thing] always happen?"
"[Type] people will understand..."
"Send this to someone who [relatable trait]..."
```

**Best For**: Lifestyle, Entertainment, Humor
**Platform Fit**: Reels (strongest for shares), TikTok

---

### 6. Authority Hooks

Establish credibility immediately:

```
"As a [profession] with [X] years experience..."
"After working with 100+ [clients]..."
"I've [impressive achievement]. Here's what I learned..."
"Having [credential], I can tell you..."
"I've spent [X] years studying [topic]..."
"[Famous person/company] paid me to [action]. Here's the truth..."
```

**Best For**: Education, Business, Professional content
**Platform Fit**: Shorts (strongest), LinkedIn, Reels

---

### 7. Fear/Urgency Hooks

Create immediate importance:

```
"You're probably making this mistake right now..."
"[X]% of people get this wrong..."
"If you're [doing thing], stop immediately..."
"This cost me [consequence]. Don't make my mistake."
"Warning: [thing] might be [problem]..."
"Before [deadline/event], you need to know..."
```

**Best For**: Finance, Health, News, Tips
**Platform Fit**: All platforms

---

### 8. Transformation Hooks

Show before/after or journey:

```
"6 months ago, I [starting point]. Now I [result]..."
"From [negative] to [positive] in [timeframe]..."
"I couldn't [thing]. Then I discovered..."
"My [area] used to be [problem]. Here's how I fixed it..."
"Watch me go from [start] to [end]..."
```

**Best For**: Tutorials, Lifestyle, Motivation
**Platform Fit**: Reels (aesthetic), Shorts

---

### 9. Number/List Hooks

Specific, scannable value:

```
"5 [things] you didn't know about [topic]"
"The 3-step system for [result]..."
"7 red flags in [context]..."
"10 seconds that changed [outcome]..."
"$0 to $[amount] doing [thing]..."
"[Specific number] people fell for this..."
```

**Best For**: Tips, Lists, Education
**Platform Fit**: All platforms

---

### 10. Mystery/Story Hooks

Narrative tension builders:

```
"So there I was, [situation]..."
"This is the craziest thing that ever happened to me..."
"What I'm about to tell you sounds fake, but..."
"Two years ago, something happened that changed everything..."
"I wasn't supposed to share this, but..."
"You'll NEVER guess what happened next..."
```

**Best For**: Entertainment, Stories, Vlogs
**Platform Fit**: TikTok (best), Shorts
